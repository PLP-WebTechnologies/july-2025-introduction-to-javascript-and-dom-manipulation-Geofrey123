Empowering Mining Communities

Who We Are

PreMCO is a registered CBO dedicated to uplifting communities through safe mining,
economic empowerment, and environmental conservation.

Economic Empowerment

We provide financial literacy, VSL training, and support small-scale entrepreneurs.

Safe Mining

We equip women miners with PPE and train them on occupational health & safety.

Environment

Tree planting, water protection, and conservation initiatives for sustainable futures.

Join Us in Transforming Lives

Together, we can build safe, sustainable, and empowered communities.
